Policy Briefs

My degree has taught me how to create a wide range of professional documents, including financial reports, Business pitches, research and analytical reports and policy briefs. Creating policy briefs gave me the skills to summarise a wide range of technical information into bite sized pieces of information that can be easily understood by a wide range of people with different understandings of the topic in question. It also refined my research skills and allowed me to use my more creative side when designing the layout of the policy brief. The visual aspect of the policy brief is equally as important as the information as it dictates how the audience will read and digest the information.
